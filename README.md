The Financial Fraud Detection Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze financial transactions, identify fraudulent activities, and provide business insights through key performance indicators (KPIs) and visualizations.

This dashboard enables users to monitor transaction trends, fraud patterns, payment methods, merchant categories, and overall fraud statistics to support data-driven decision-making.

# Objectives
Monitor total transactions and transaction amount.
Track fraudulent transactions and fraud losses.
Analyze monthly transaction trends.
Identify payment methods with the highest fraud.
Analyze fraud distribution across merchant categories.
Compare genuine vs fraudulent transactions.
Enable interactive filtering by Year, Month, and Location.

# Key Insights
Fraudulent transactions account for approximately 9.6% of total transactions.
Debit Card and UPI transactions show the highest number of fraud cases.
Food and Entertainment categories experience the most fraudulent activity.
Monthly transaction volumes fluctuate throughout the year, highlighting seasonal trends.
Genuine transactions represent over 90% of all recorded transactions.
